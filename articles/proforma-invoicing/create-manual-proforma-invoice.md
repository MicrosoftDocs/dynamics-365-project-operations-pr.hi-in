### <a name="edit-a-draft-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="39f2e-164">ड्राफ्ट इनवॉइस संपादित करें</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="39f2e-164">Edit a draft invoice</span></span>

### <a name="correct-a-confirmed-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="39f2e-176">एक पुष्टि किए गए इनवॉइस को ठीक करें</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="39f2e-176">Correct a confirmed invoice</span></span>
