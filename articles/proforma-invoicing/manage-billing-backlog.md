## <a name="time-and-material-billing-backlog"></a><span data-ttu-id="86f4d-122">समय और सामग्री बिलिंग बैकलॉग</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="86f4d-122">Time and Material Billing Backlog</span></span>
